Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes Farming Guide

This guide is designed to help a beginning player have the best start in Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes:

  • Strong and Balanced Roster for Grand Arena Championships
  • Join a Guild Participating in:
    • The Pit Raid
    • Tank Takedown Raid
    • Hoth: Light Side Territory Battles
    • Geonosis: Dark Side Territory Battles
  • Rebel Light Side and Empire Dark Side Teams for Mission Progress
  • Bounty Hunter Team for Credit Heist/Training Droid Events
  • Jedi Team for Galactic War Progress
  • Several Journey Guide Characters
  • Separatist Fleet for Early Fleet Arena Crystals/Zetas
  • Executor Capital Ship for Fleet Dominance
  • Galactic Legend Sith Eternal Emperor

Phase 0

This is the start of the game. From here you will:

  1. Begin Farming a Light Side Squad for Early Mission Progress
  2. Start Farming Old Ben to 7 stars to Join Heroic Tier Raids Once You Join a Guild
No Need to Farm to 7 stars for Ahsoka Tano and Jedi Knight Anakin. Farm enough to Advance to Next Phase.

Phase 1

In this phase you will be looking to join a guild and enter Squad Arena, while beginning farming requirements for your first Journey Guide characters:

  1. Join a guild and start farming Sun Fac
  2. Enter Squad Arena
  3. Switch farming focus over to Journey Guide character requirements
    • R2D2: Empire
    • Emperor Palpatine: Rebels
    • Commander Luke Skywalker: Old Ben, Farmboy Luke, Stormtrooper Han, Princess Leia, R2D2
  4. Farm Boba Fett from Cantina enough to complete the first few tiers of the Credit Heist and Training Droid events
  5. Start farming Geonosion Soldier from Cantina Battles
  6. Build a Rebel Team and an Empire Team
Your main focus here is to advance your light side squad to battle 4-C for General Veers. Farm him just as needed here until you get to light side 6-C. Your secondary focus is to advance your dark side squad to 3-A for Range Trooper.

Phase 2

Phase 2 consists of advancing levels while farming characters to complete a few very helpful events and beginning to farm for your fleet squad:

  1. Farm more Bounty Hunters
  2. Farm pilots for fleet
  3. Farm ships
Gear characters as needed to complete events and battles. Your goal is to complete events and continue to gear your Commander Luke Skywalker and Emperor Palpatine requirements.

Phase 3

In Phase 3, you will switch focus to farming and gearing a Jedi team to be able to complete Galactic War every day, while still gearing up your Commander Luke Skywalker and Emperor Palpatine requirements:

  1. Farm and gear Jedi to complete Galactic War daily
  2. After unlocking Galactic War, keep all tokens earned to purchase ships
  3. Gear pilots and farm ships to place high in Fleet Arena
  4. Unlock Fleets and compete in Fleet Arena daily
  5. Focus the Executrix Capital Ship and Grand Moff Tarkin
  6. Gear up a Jedi team to unlock Grand Master Yoda
  7. Unlock Darth Vader through achievements
By the end of this phase you should be able to complete Galactic War and a few Fleet Challenges. Begin working through Fleet Battles as well.

Phase 4

Phase 4 is the last phase before reaching level 85 and being able to compete in Grand Arena Championships. In this phase your main goals are:

  1. Finish off your fleet squads
  2. Have unlocked Commander Luke Skywalker, Emperor Palpatine, and Grand Master Yoda from the Journey Guide
  3. Build 6 strong squads for GAC
    • CLS Rebels
    • Empire
    • Bounty Hunters
    • Jedi
    • Imperial Troopers
    • Geonosions
  4. Begin farming Executor requirements
After you reach level 85, your next goal will be to unlock Executor, which can win GAC matches all on its own and compete for first place in Fleet Arena daily.
